Mixer Legit Or Scam Lookup

If you are about to send coins to a mixer that does not appear on the primary BitMixList, run it through this lookup first. The tool queries the ScamWhammer database I curate so you can spot phishing clones, seized services, and copycats before a single satoshi leaves your wallet.

Type the host name only (no https://) and ScamWhammer will immediately tell you whether that mixer has been reported as a scam. It works for clearnet and onion links, and everything runs inside your browser for maximum privacy.

Once a domain clears this lookup, head back to the Bitcoin Mixer List to review its uptime, letters of guarantee, and Tor mirrors before mixing.
